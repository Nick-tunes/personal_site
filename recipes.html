This is my Favorite Recipe!

  • 9 RED food coloring drops

  • 2 cups of flour

  • 4 tbsp WHITE chocolate chips

  • 3 BROWN eggs

  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

  • 25 grams of sugar

  • 2 tbsp butter

  • 1 pinch of LOVE (•‿•)

Instructions:

Pre-heat your oven to 425º. Prepare a baking sheet with parchment paper. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl. Mix wet ingredients in another. Combine all ingredients until clumps are gone. Wash your hands before you touch the dough. Roll little 1 inch balls of your dough and place them on your baking sheet. Bake at 425º for 10 minutes or until golden brown. Enjoy your Secret Code Cookies!
